local's own market

Overview

Redesign of a website for a local business, focusing on increasing usability, highlighting the company, and improving the overall user experience.

Role

Interior Page Design, Comps, Coding, Client Interviews

Skills

HTML, CSS, Prototyping, Interviewing

Software

Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop

project goals

Through our redesign of the Local’s Own Market website, my team and I aimed to create an online presence that highlighted the function of the company while still communicating the mission & values of the business. We also strived to create a user-friendly interface with simple navigation and a thought-through site structure.

information architecture

Before designing any visual aspects, I took inventory of the important information and pages required and developed an information architecture for the site.

company integrity

The company’s mission was extremely important to them, so we designed a site that made sure to make their purpose clear while also making it clear to the user how the site operates.
