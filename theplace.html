the place

Overview

Develop a website for a local makers’ space and bring their physical environment to life in their online presence.

Role

Research, Design, Mobile/Desktop Prototypes, Coding

Skills

HTML, CSS, Prototyping, Interviewing

Software

Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop

project goals

After meeting with the client and touring their property, I set out to develop a site that would embody their organization and members in a digital aspect. There was not an existing site and very little information to go off of, and a large portion of the project was spend brainstorming, mapping, and planning.

mind mapping

After individual reflections and planning, I formed a team and we brought together our ideas and thoughts through a mind-mapping session. We wrote down everything that came to mind about the client and categorized them to create a clearer picture of what our design should encompass.

prototyping

After detailing the information we wanted to present on the site, I began creating several different design mockups and prototypes, fo both the mobile and desktop versions.

bringing it together

My team and I brought together our different prototypes and created a single design for our site. I then migrated my prototypes to code and worked with my team members to link all of the pieces into one cohesive site.
