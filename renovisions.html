renovisions

Overview

Developing a product concept for home renovation-focused CAD software designed to be both user-friendly for the average homeowner and a powerful visualization tool.

Role

Customer Interviews, Problem-Need Identification, Journey Mapping, Solution Development, Business Model Development, Engineering Requirements, Interface Design, Wireframing, User Testing, Product Documentation

Skills

User Research, Market Analysis, Experience Design, Prototyping, Technical Writing, Concept Presentation, Project Management, Product Development

Software

SketchUp, SolidWorks, Unity, Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign

project goals

The objective for this project was to design a product that acts as a solution to key problems and market gaps in the home renovation industry. A purposely vague initial objective allowed for the development of a problem/need-based solution that is highly tailored to customer desires.

market research &
competitive analysis

In the early stages of the project, I spent a lot of time exploring existing solutions and going through the experience of using those products. I put myself in the shoes of a homeowner planning a renovation and went through the process of using SketchUp to create a detailed model of renovation plans for an actual home. I tracked the amount of time that was required to learn and use the software for the desired purpose.

research & interviews

I interviewed several industry professionals and renovation-experienced homeowners to learn more about their experiences and identify problems and needs. As a result of this research, I was able to identify the primary problems I wanted my solution to address.

developing the solution

After in-depth research, analysis, and mind-mapping, I landed on a product concept that addressed they key requirements I had identified.

balancing business & design

Throughout the product design process, I created and maintained a business model canvas in order to keep the product development in-line with realistic market opportunities.

prototyping

As I continue developing this product concept, I am working to outline the core software features through mockups and wireframes. I am currently in the process of expanding my prototype and conducting user testing.
