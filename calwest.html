california west

Overview

Website and branding redesign for a local property management company.

Role

Research, Design, Prototyping, Coding

Skills

HTML, CSS, Prototyping

Software

Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop

project goals

For this website redesign, I set out to create a modern layout that would simplify the site’s appearance, information, and navigation. I aimed to retain the important features and branding of the original site while also modernizing the company’s presence to fit their typical audience.

mockups

I created mockups for the home and interior pages of the site in order to form a cohesive theme and grid that would flow nicely across all pages.

migrating to code

I printed out my completed mockups and traced layouts above them in order to “div out” the different sections. After sectioning off the pages according to their html structure, it made migrating the design to code much more simple and efficient.

merging old & new

Although my redesigned site had a completely new look and structure than the original, I emphasized retaining the important information and pages from the original. My completed site was still distinctly the same company, even with an entirely new look.
