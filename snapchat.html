snapchat redesign

Overview

User-centered redesign of the Snapchat mobile app, following an unpopular UI update.

Role

User Research & Testing, UI/UX Design, Prototyping

Skills

Prototyping, User Research, User Testing

Software

Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop

project goals

As a design experiment, I was tasked with redesigning the Snapchat mobile app. The project was completely user research and experience based, as it was intended to create a solution for the poor user experience introduced through a recent update. After conducting interviews and research, I created a prototype of my design and tested it with regular Snapchat users.

usability

One of my main objectives was to increase organization and usability throughout the app. I addressed complaints of cluttered screens through the use of tabs and content sections.

customization

While the Snapchat users I interviewed all used the app in relatively the same way, they all wanted more control over the content in their feeds. I created several features that would allow users to manage the information in different content sections.

familiarity

Although the goal of this project was to redesign the app, I made sure to retain (and bring back) several of the key features that users love and have used for years. I focused on enhancing an experience for users, and achieved it through a balance of familiar and new.
