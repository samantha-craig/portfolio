sacramento passageway

Overview

Creation of a virtual reality model to illustrate the redesign of the Sacramento K-Street passageway.

Role

VR Model Development, Specializing in Textures, Materials, & Game Objects

Skills

Project Management, Version Control, Interdisciplanary Design

Software

Unity, GitHub, Vuforia, Maya, Photoshop

project goals

My team and I began this project with a blank, grey-scale model of the passageway. In the span of 10 weeks, we aimed to add textures, animations, lighting, landscaping, art, sound, and realistic details to the model. I specialized in textures, with the goal of adding base materials to the floors and walls.

my role: textures

We edited our scene in Unity, and the texturing process was quite complex. The model was mostly one imported piece, and I had to create texture planes against every surface we needed to texture. I then created a new material, added textures and mapping to it, and applied it to the texture plane.

creating the foliage

In addition to texture planes, I created the textures for the trees my teammates designed. The foliage required several layers of textures, mapping, and materials, and was customized for each individual tree.

landscaping

When creating the passageway environment, I both matched the materials in the existing tunnel and added new improvements to the scene. I re-created this planter with lush landscaping to add vibrance to the scene.
